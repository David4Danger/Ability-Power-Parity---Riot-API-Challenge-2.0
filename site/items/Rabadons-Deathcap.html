Summary: Rabadon's Deathcap had a slightly lower winrate and popularity after the adjustments made to it, but these numbers aren't significant enough to consider Rabadon's worse off. More significantly, in both wins and losses, games where Rabadon's was built end 2.64% quicker after the changes to Rabadon's were applied. This implies that summoners are building Rabadon's earlier, but it isn't creating much of a difference in the items winrate itself.

"Additionally, since Deathcap is now the "I want lots of AP" item, we figured we could push it a little harder." This is still true while the item is slightly less popular, since people are now opting for more variety in their AP builds. Riot making sure we can still nuke squishies!

Viktor built Rabadon's 40.88% of the time in patch 5.11.
Viktor built Rabadon's 43.31% of the time in patch 5.14.
Viktor built Rabadon's 2.43% more after it was updated.

Viktor saw a good increase in the popularity of Rabadon's deathcap, and while the items overall popularity went down, Viktor had a strong net gain on Rabadon's built per game.

Ahri built Rabadon's 39.47% of the time in patch 5.11.
Ahri built Rabadon's 40.94% of the time in patch 5.14.
Ahri built Rabadon's 1.47% more after it was updated.

Ahri found a minor increase in Rabadon's popularity despite an overall drop in the item's popularity. This increase could be proportional to the decrease in Luden's Echo popularity on Ahri, as some players might just be seeking the massive flat AP Rabadon's provides that Luden's no longer can.

Karthus built Rabadon's 48.36% of the time in patch 5.11.
Karthus built Rabadon's 46.75% of the time in patch 5.14.
Karthus built Rabadon's -1.61% less after it was updated.

Karthus had a minor decrease in the popularity of Rabadon's after the changes. This is good as it may imply that Karthus players are diversifying their builds with other items that were changed in this patch.

Veigar built Rabadon's 60.63% of the time in patch 5.11.
Veigar built Rabadon's 63.17% of the time in patch 5.14.
Veigar built Rabadon's 2.54% more after it was updated.

The Tiny Master of Evil couldn't resist the urge to build Rabadon's even more after the update to Rabadon's. This item in particular makes every Q really count, for an extra 0.35 AP as it happens. Every three Qs you land, it is almost like getting a fourth for free!

Syndra built Rabadon's 44.21% of the time in patch 5.11.
Syndra built Rabadon's 46.73% of the time in patch 5.14.
Syndra built Rabadon's 2.52% more after it was updated.

Most of the champions studied for the Rabadon's category show an increase in popularity, yet overall Rabadon's popularity fell. This suggests that most of the champions analyzed on this page are well suited for building this item - Syndra is not an exception.

Heimerdinger built Rabadon's 46.43% of the time in patch 5.11.

Piltover's finest couldn't resist the urge to power up his inventions even more with the new Rabadon's. Indeed, a wise choice.

Sometimes, you just need more damage. It seems that in Russia, that time is quite often. In patch 5.11, Russia had an average 1.45 Rabadon's Deathcaps built per game, compared to the global average of 1.04 per game. For patch 5.14, the Russian average decreased to 1.34 Rabadon's Deathcaps per game, compared to the global average of 1.01 per game.

Overall, Russia decreased more than the overall global average did, so Russian summoners are defintely diversifying their builds a bit more than previously. Good work, Russia!
