Will of the Ancients was changed in attempt to make it more of a "stable" item. Avoiding resistances is convenient but for the slightly lower flat spellvamp, this item went from unpopular to almost nonexistent, going from being built every 4 games to every 5. A popularity rate this low among the covered AP items is pretty much unheard of, with the patch 5.11 Nashor's Tooth even scoring higher. While the items winrate hasn't really dipped all that much, it is all too clear that summoners aren't interested in the new change to Will of the Ancients.

See those three guys to the right? Those are pretty much the only people left who build Will of the Ancients. :(

Vladimir built Will of the Ancients 91.06% of the time in patch 5.11.

Vladimir built Will of the Ancients -0.55% less after it was updated.

Before the changes were made to WOTA, it was a staple item that pretty much every Vlad player built, no matter the remainder of their build path. The changes to spellvamp with this item haven't deterred this at all, but it is interesting to compare Vlad versus the item in general. The difference between the global average, -29.63%, and the Vlad average, -0.55, indicates that the item only stayed relevant for the most iconic builds. That means less diversity than before :(
