Summary: Void Staff recieved very minimal changes just to keep up with the theme of the patch by making AP items more potent in order to provide diversity. As seen above... wait, really? You aren't seeing things, Void staff went up a full 2% in winrate with just 10 additional AP for some extra gold. It was also 25% less popular after being updated in patch 5.13. This leads to the question of just how much of these statistics are independant of games where Void Staff is built. This paints a good picture about how successful this patch was for increasing item diversity. Void staff is never your first item, it fits the role of support staff in most AP item builds.

A conclusion can be drawn from this; with a higher diversity in AP items, popularity of a background AP item like this falls as other options are explored. It is most likely that summoners are only reaching Void Staff as their last item, which has an inherent bias towards a higher win rate. More AP options = more build variety!
