Rod of Ages, like many items this patch that weren't in that 120AP trinity, just got more potent. As seen above, Rod of Ages boasts the fastest game times of any AP item changed in this patch. Before being updated it was already incredibly short, but with the changes in sustainability and increased offensive capabilities, the rod is making games all the quicker. It suffered small hits in winrate and popularity, indicating that while these changes are making Rod of Ages games quicker, they aren't necessarily winning these fast games.

"We want to tune up more of Rod of Ages' offensive AP / mana capabilities", mission accomplished! Is it cold on the rift? Because we're getting a lot of snowball action with these changes.

Rod of Ages had the 2nd shortest average game length during wins of any item on any patch, clocking in at an average gametime of 19.84 minutes in Korea on patch 5.14.

Any sub-20 gametime indicates that a lot of games are won even before the surrender option is available. This could be made up of a combination of games snowballing all the time, and the "open mid" phase of games gone bad is a lot more prevalent in Korea.
